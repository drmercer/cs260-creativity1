CS-260 Hackathon 2017

Think you have what it takes to be the class champ? Prove it!

About

CS-260 is a web programming course offered at Brigham Young University. In order to encourage students to apply the skills they are learning, we will be holding our first ever hackathon for this course. Students may feel free to be creative with their projects and show off their skills to the class — as well as the professor, Mark Clement.

Contact us here: clement@cs.byu.edu

Rules

  • You may work with other students, but you must write your own code. (Just like traditional CS labs.)
  • You may code in any language.
  • You must submit your source code with your solutions to the problems.
  • The contest will end at 11:59pm on the last day of class, 14 Dec 2017.
  • You may not share code or distribute any problems or answers anywhere on the internet.

Enter your name and email address to sign up for the competition!
